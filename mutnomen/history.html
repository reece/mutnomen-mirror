History regarding the description of sequence variants


Last modified January 11, 2013

Publications on the description of sequence variants

  • 1993
    • Beaudet AL, Tsui LC (1993). A suggested nomenclature for designating mutations. Hum.Mutat. 2 (4): 245-248. PMID:8401532.
    • Beutler E (1993). The designation of mutations. Am.J.Hum.Genet. 53 (3): 783-785. PMID:8352285.
  • 1994
    • Antonarakis SE, McKusick VA (1994). Discussion on mutation nomenclature. Hum.Mutat. 4 (2): 166. PMID:7981723.
  • 1996
    • Ad Hoc Committee on Mutation Nomenclature (1996). Update on nomenclature for human gene mutations. Hum.Mutat. 8 (3): 197-202. PMID:8889576.
    • Beutler E, McKusick VA, Motulsky AG, Scriver CR, Hutchinson F (1996). Mutation nomenclature: nicknames, systematic names, and unique identifiers. Hum.Mutat. 8 (3): 203-206. PMID:8889577.
  • 1998
    • Antonarakis SE (1998). Recommendations for a nomenclature system for human gene mutations. Hum.Mutat. 11 (1): 1-3. PMID:9450896.
      Original discussion document.
  • 2000
    • den Dunnen JT, Antonarakis SE (2000). Mutation nomenclature extensions and suggestions to describe complex mutations: a discussion. Hum.Mutat. 15 (1): 7-12. PMID:10612815.
      A modified version of this paper was published as; den Dunnen JT, Antonarakis SE (2001). Nomenclature for the description of human sequence variations. Hum.Genet. 109 (1): 121-124. PMID:11479744.
  • 2003
    • den Dunnen JT (2003). Mutation nomenclature. Nature Encyclopedia of the Human Genome 211-214.
  • 2003
    • den Dunnen JT, Antonarakis SE (2003). Mutation nomenclature. Current Protocols in Human Genetics 7.13.1-7.13.8.
  • 2004
    • den Dunnen JT, Paalman MH (2004). Standardizing mutation nomenclature: why bother?. Hum.Mutat. 22 (3): 181-182. PMID:12938082.
  • 2011
    • Taschner PEM, den Dunnen JT (2011). Describing structural changes by extending HGVS sequence variation nomenclature. Hum.Mutat. 32 (5): 507-511. PMID:21309030.
