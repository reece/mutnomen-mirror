Exon entirely in intron

Detailed expression analysis of this fictive gene showed that a minor fraction of the transcripts (<5%) contained an alternatively spliced exon located entirely inside intron 53. Based on a coding DNA reference sequence, numbering of these differentially spliced nucleotides is straightforward, i.e. as for the intronic nucleotides c.9660+290 to c.9660+319. The encoded amino acids can best be numbered p.Pro3220+1 to p.Gln3220+10; this number clearly indicates their position in relation to the other amino acids with the '+' number indicating a location in an intron (see below).
